Detailed Product Description
Micro Step-Down Converter, Adjustable
If you want to work with high current, ensure proper heat dissipation!

According to available information, there are modules on the market that are not equipped with the original chip and can cause damage to the electronics connected to such a module. The modules we supply are equipped with the original chip marked |AGC(N).

Specifications:
Control Circuit: MP2315
Input Voltage: DC 4.5-24V
Output Voltage: Adjustable and fixed output
Adjustable Range: 0.8-17V, fixed voltage 1.8V 2.5V 3.3V 5V 9V 12V
Output Current: 3A (max)
Efficiency: up to 97.5% (in 6.5V out 5V 0.7A)
Switching Frequency: 500KHz
Ripple Wave: 20mV (in 12V out 5V 3A)
Overvoltage Protection: no
Static Current: 0.85mA
Dimensions: 20x11x5mm

The default output voltage is adjustable, if you need a fixed output voltage, cut the path shown in the picture and then connect the contacts (1.8, 2.5, 3.3, 5, 9, 12V).
